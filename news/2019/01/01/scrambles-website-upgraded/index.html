Scramble’s Website Upgraded

When we first set up Scramble’s website, we used wordpress.com for its simplicity and affordability, at the address scrambletheduck.wordpress.com. However, through the past year, the shortcomings of wordpress.com (notably the increasing advertisements and price for a custom domain) for a specialized website such as Scramble’s have made a new, more customizable website (scrambletheduck.org) desirable.

We will leave the scrambletheduck.wordpress.com site up, but its content will not be updated. From now on, all updates and new information will be posted exclusively to scrambletheduck.org, and all pertinent content from the old site has been transferred to the new site.
