Scramble to be Featured in Eastford Parade

Scramble the Duck will be in the Eastford Memorial Day Parade on May 28. The Parade will start at 9:30 a.m. at the Eastford Elementary School (12 Westford Rd.), and end at the Ivy Glenn Memorial (179 Rt. 198) where Scramble makes his predictions. Following the parade will be a ceremony. Scramble will be alongside veterans, the East Woodstock Cornet Band, Fire Trucks, Antique cars and more. In case of rain, the parade will be canceled and the ceremony will be held in the School’s gym. If you haven’t met Scramble yet, this is a great opportunity to meet him in person.

Read the parade announcement on the Eastford town website.
