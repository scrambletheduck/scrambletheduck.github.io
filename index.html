Welcome to Scramble the Duck's website! Scramble predicts the weather every February 2, at 7:30 A.M. at the Ivy Glenn Memorial in Eastford, CT in a free event. Scramble predicts the weather somewhat like a groundhog, looking for a shadow to determine whether there will be an early spring or six more weeks of winter. However, he is 100% accurate, compared to Punxsutawney Phil's 39%. On February 2, he predicted six more weeks of winter.

Scramble is correct again!
